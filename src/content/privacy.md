##### Introduction
Phaeno Inc. (“Company”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

This policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit the websites www.phaenobiotech.com, portal.phaenobiotech.com, or any other phaenobiotech.com domains (collectively, our “Website”) and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

This policy applies to information we collect:
- On this Website;
- In email, text, and other electronic messages between you and this Website;
- When you interact with our advertising and applications on third-party websites and services, if those applications or advertising include links to this policy.

It does **not** apply to information collected by us offline, through any other means, or by any third party (including our affiliates and subsidiaries).

By accessing or using the Website, you agree to this privacy policy. This policy may change from time to time (see “Changes to Our Privacy Policy”).

##### Important Note – Nature of Our Service
Phaeno is a **research and bioinformatics analysis platform only**. We do **not** accept clinical specimens, diagnostic samples, or any data containing protected health information (PHI), patient identifiers, or direct identifiers of research participants. Users are solely responsible for ensuring that any human-origin genetic or genomic data uploaded to the platform has been appropriately de-identified or anonymized in accordance with applicable laws, regulations, and ethical guidelines before upload. We process all uploaded data as non-clinical research data.

##### Children Under the Age of 13
Our Website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

##### Information We Collect and How We Collect It
We collect:
- Personal information (e.g., name, work address, email, phone);
- Non-identifying information (e.g., employer, job title);
- Technical and usage data (e.g., IP address, browser type, pages visited).

We collect this information directly from you, automatically via cookies and similar technologies, and occasionally from third-party business partners.

Uploaded research data may include genetic sequencing data (human or non-human) and accompanying metadata.

##### Automatic Data Collection Technologies
We use cookies, web storage, web beacons, and Google Analytics (with anonymized User-IDs) to improve the Website and your experience. You may disable cookies in your browser, but some features may stop working.

##### How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
- Provide and operate the Website and our services;
- Perform analytics, troubleshooting, and system monitoring;
- Communicate with you about your account and our services;
- Fulfill contractual obligations;
- Comply with legal requirements; and
- With your consent, send you marketing communications about our own or selected third-party offerings.

##### Data Retention
We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services, fulfill the purposes in this policy, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You may request deletion at any time.

##### How We Share and Disclose Your Information
We **do not sell** personal information. We may share personal information:
- With your explicit consent;
- With affiliates, contractors, and service providers bound by confidentiality;
- In connection with a merger, sale of assets, or similar transaction;

Aggregated or de-identified data may be shared without restriction.

##### International Data Transfers
